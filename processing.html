Tetris

*Only playable with a keyboard*

An attempt of making the classic game, Tetris. Arrow keys to move side to side. Up key to rotate. Down key to descend quicker. Shift to swap tiles.

  • Source code: Tetris

Snake

*Only playable with a keyboard*

An attempt of making the classic game, Snake. Arrow keys to move.

  • Source code: Snake

Satellite

*Only playable with a mouse & keyboard*

Visualizer based on satellites around celestial bodies. Left click to create anchor, left click anchor to convert to crawling anchor. Right click and hold to create anchor on cursor. Right click anchor to remove. "W" key to toggle walls.

  • Source code: Satellite

PolyMorph

*Only playable with a mouse*

Visualizer of warping polygon shapes. Click to add vertex.

  • Source code: PolyMorph

Pretty Colours

*Only playable with a keyboard*

Visualizer based on water ripples. Click mouse to create ripple. Hold down mouse and drag to create water strider.

  • Source code: Pretty Colours

Fractal Tree

*Only playable with a mouse*

Visualizer of a fractal tree. Move cursor up/down to increase branch angles.

  • Source code: Fractal Tree

Dot Grid

*Only playable with a mouse*

Visualizer of simple gravity grid. Click and hold to repel instead of attract. All points are equally affected in bottom canvas.

  • Source code: Dot Grid 1, Dot Grid 2
